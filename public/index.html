Being a very visual person, I need a way to "lay out" my life in front of me in order to have a sense of self.

And since social media nowadays just sucks, my growing rage and disgust for these platforms led me to lose said sense of self, since i could not bring myself to use them (still struggling), so it was either continuing to feel like im in a black hole or doing something about it, looking for solutions.

So essentially, I built this to visualize my life in a more convenient way since no social media app has all the features I want.

Im also planning on letting out the voices in the form of essays from time to time cause I dont wanna cram my Obsidian too much.

Stick around maybe I'm actually gonna write and design on here and this is not gonna end up as yet another failed attempt at keeping a hobby.
